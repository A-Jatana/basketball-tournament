Rules & Details

  • 3v3 Half-Court Play (Two games running simultaneously).
  • Game Length: 15 minutes per half, 5-minute halftime.
  • Scoring: Regular scoring (2 and 3 points).
  • No Free Throws: Fouls result in a check ball at the top.
  • Team Size: Minimum 3 players, Maximum 5 players.
  • Intermediate to Advanced level only.
