Rules & Details

  • Games will follow a 3v3 half-court format.
  • Each game consists of two halves, each lasting 15 minutes.
  • A 5-minute halftime break will be given between halves.
  • 2 points for regular field goals and 3 points for shots made beyond the arc.
  • Fouls result in a check ball at the top of the key, no free throws
  • Each team must have a minimum of 3 players and a maximum of 5 players.
  • Substitutions can be made at any time during a dead ball (when play is stopped).
  • This tournament is designed for players at an intermediate to advanced skill level.
